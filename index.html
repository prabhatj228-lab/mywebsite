Research Proposal · Apr 2026

Evaluating India's PLI Scheme & Import Dependency

Designed a firm-level Difference-in-Differences framework to study industrial policy impact on import intensity and value addition. Integrated CMIE Prowess, trade tariff, and policy data within a panel setting (2015–2025). Addressed identification issues including selection bias, concurrent policies, and parallel trends.

DiD Framework Panel Data CMIE Prowess Causal Inference
Instructor: Dr. Sandeep Kumar Aggarwal, IISER Bhopal

Two-decade analysis of unemployment and inflation trends in India. Tested validity of the Phillips Curve using econometric techniques — found short-run trade-off exists but breaks down in the long run, supporting the vertical long-run Phillips Curve hypothesis.
